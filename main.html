About Tablets

Tablets are portable touchscreen devices that blend the functionality of smartphones and laptops. They are used for media consumption, productivity, education, and gaming.

Types of Tablets

Traditional Tablets

Standard touchscreen devices for browsing, media, and apps. Examples: iPad, Samsung Galaxy Tab.

2-in-1 Tablets

Convert into laptops with detachable keyboards. Examples: Microsoft Surface Pro, Lenovo Yoga Book.

Gaming Tablets

Designed for gaming with high-performance specs. Examples: Asus ROG Flow Z13, iPad Pro.

Budget Tablets

Affordable tablets for basic use. Examples: Amazon Fire, Samsung A Series.

Top Tablet Companies

  • Apple: Known for iPads with the iPadOS ecosystem.
  • Samsung: Offers Galaxy Tab series with Android OS.
  • Microsoft: Provides Surface series with Windows OS.
  • Amazon: Affordable Fire tablets with a custom FireOS.
  • Lenovo: Creates budget-friendly and business tablets.

Pricing Comparison

Most Affordable

Amazon Fire HD: Starting at $60, ideal for basic needs.

Most Expensive

iPad Pro (12.9-inch): Can cost up to $2,000 with max specs.

Best Value

Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 Lite: Affordable with strong performance.
